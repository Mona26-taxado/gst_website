About Us- Grahak Sahaayata Kendra

In this digital age, where everything in business has gone digital, we at Grahak Sahaayata Kendra provide an easy, efficient, and long-term solution to all your multi-utility payment and document needs. We abide by the Digital India norms in the best possible way. Our main aim is to channelize the payments to unified digital platforms where you get all the security and convenience. Our services range widely.

We provide applications for applying for a driving license, passport, voter ID card, state certificate, AEPS (Aadhaar enabled payment system), etc. All these facilities are required at one time or another in life and some are even required at regular intervals. This is why we have made an application, a one-stop solution that would take care of all your needs and would not need you to scour the internet to reach the form you need. You can rely on us for providing easy solutions to all your problems.
